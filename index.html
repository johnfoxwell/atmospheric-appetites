About Us

We are a small team of students who are working on our first project. We each come from different backgrounds, but are using our newly found skills to work together and create an innovative new website. We spent time going over different ideas of something unique that we could do, and this stood out to all of us.

Have you ever been trying to find a recipe and there is either too many ideas or not enough ideas? Well this site aims to fix that problem for you. We have created a recipe finder that uses your input location to figure out what dish is right for you in that moment. We grab the current weather for your given area, and then we have a set of dishes that it could randomly generate for you based of off the temperature outside. For a cold day, you could be looking to make a nice warm stew. On a hot day, you might want something light and refreshing. This site will help you find a great meal that goes perfectly with how your body may be feeling.
